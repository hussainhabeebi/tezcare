Frequently Asked Questions

Clear Answers, No Surprises.

How quickly can TezCare provide a worker?

Availability depends on the role, location, shift time, notice provided and whether a suitably screened worker is available. We review urgent requests promptly, but only confirm a placement once an appropriate worker has been identified.

How do you check your workers?

Checks may include identity and right-to-work verification, employment history and references, DBS status, relevant training and, where applicable, professional registration. The checks completed depend on the role and your requirements.

What happens if a confirmed worker becomes unavailable?

We let you know as soon as possible and make reasonable efforts to find a suitable replacement. While we can't guarantee a replacement will always be available, keeping you informed early is our priority.

How do I get your rates?

Contact our team for the current TezCare client rate card. Rates may vary by role, shift, notice period, bank holiday and placement requirements.
